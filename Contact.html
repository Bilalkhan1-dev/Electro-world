Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and mobile payment solutions. We also offer flexible installment plans for selected products to make premium electronics more accessible.

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes, we provide free delivery services throughout Karachi for orders above PKR 50,000. For smaller orders and locations outside Karachi, delivery charges may apply based on distance and product size.

What is your return and warranty policy?

We offer a 7-day return policy for most products in original condition. All our products come with manufacturer warranties ranging from 1-5 years depending on the brand and product category. We also provide extended warranty options.

Do you provide installation services?

Yes, we provide professional installation services for air conditioners, TVs, washing machines, and other appliances. Our certified technicians ensure proper installation with a 1-year service warranty.

Can I check product availability before visiting?

Absolutely! Call us at +92 300 2263673 or send a WhatsApp message to check product availability, pricing, and reserve items before your visit. This saves your time and ensures product availability.

Do you offer trade-in services?

Yes, we accept trade-ins for select categories like air conditioners, refrigerators, and TVs. Our experts will evaluate your old appliance and offer a fair trade-in value towards your new purchase.
